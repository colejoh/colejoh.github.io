Purpose
CodeRed is ever-growing and improving, and this growth requires active student involvement and assistance. CodeRed exists to foster community in the Computer Science Department. The goals of the board include:

• Creating frequent hack nights that include discovering emerging technology

• Hack nights must include a unique food theme (ie Raspberry Pi(e) til You Die)

• Provide an educational environment to stem academic growth

• Foster a growing hacker culture
